<div class="jumbotron-tagline">In the wake of its shattering, the world is born anew.</div>

<div class="intro-txt"><p>Seventy years ago, the barrier between our world and theirs shattered. <a href="/wiki/world:mythics">Monsters</a> and <a href="wiki/world:magic">magic</a> ravaged humanity, tearing it apart.</p>

<p>But humanity will not go easily.</p>

<p>Now, a thriving <a href="/wiki/world:cradle">settlement</a> of thousands has built a home in the valley of British Columbia, protected by the towering mountains and the <a href="/wiki/world:wardens">Wardens</a> who watch their passes. Mages who have bound themselves to the very monsters who seek to destroy us, the Wardens have vowed to use their magic and their bond to keep humanity safe - even if it means their own minds may shatter in the process.</p>

<div class="jumbotron-tagline">Shattered will open its doors January 22.</div>
