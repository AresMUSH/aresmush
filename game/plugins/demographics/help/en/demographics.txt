Demographics commands are used to set your basic character info.  Some items may not be changed after your character has been approved.

    %xcfullname <name>%xn
    %xcgender <male/female/other>%xn
    %xcheight <height>%xn
    %xcphysique <build/body type - athletic, wiry, slim, pudgy, etc.>%xn
    %xchair <hair color>%xn
    %xceyes <eye color>%xn
    %xcskin <complexion>%xn
    %xcbirthdate <mm/dd/yyyy>%xn or %xcage <years>%xn
    %xcreputation <reputation>%xn - Information that you want people to know about you ICly.
    %xccallsign <name>%xn - Callsign for pilots and other military chars.
