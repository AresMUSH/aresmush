A few final touches.

You'll want to set your description - don't walk around naked.

It's fun to imagine the characters on a game like characters in a movie, and to pick actors to play them.  The actors system lets you keep track of who's picked which actors, and pick one for yourself.

%xh-- Give it a try! --%xn 

    Type: %xchelp describe%xn for desc commands and set your desc.
    Type: %xchelp actors%xn for actors commands and pick an actor.
